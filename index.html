The Beauty of Simple Webpages

Published: March 1, 2026

A simple webpage can still feel alive and expressive. With thoughtful colours, smooth shadows, and clean typography, even a single article layout can feel modern and inviting.

Vibrant design doesn’t require complexity—just a few well‑chosen styles. A warm gradient, rounded corners, and subtle animation can transform a plain page into something memorable.

As you continue exploring HTML and CSS, you’ll discover how small touches can dramatically improve the user experience while keeping your code clean and approachable.
